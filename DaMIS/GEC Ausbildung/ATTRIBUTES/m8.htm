Attribut: materiel-materiel-association-status-description-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that indicates if the status of a specific MATERIEL-MATERIEL-ASSOCIATION-STATUS refers to the beginning or termination of the association.
Attributwerte
End association The period of effectiveness of the specified association is ending.
Start association The period of effectiveness of the specified association is beginning.
